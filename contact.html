Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and consultations.

How do I book a consultation?

You can book a consultation by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or using the booking form on our consultations page. We'll get back to you within 24 hours to confirm your appointment.

What should I expect during my first consultation?

Your first consultation will include a comprehensive health assessment, review of your medical history, discussion of current symptoms, and development of a personalized treatment plan with herbal recommendations.

Are your herbal products safe?

Yes, all our products are made with natural ingredients and undergo rigorous quality control. However, we always recommend consulting with our herbalists to ensure the products are suitable for your specific needs.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online consultations via video call. Online consultations are just as comprehensive and effective as in-person sessions.

How long does shipping take for products?

Local delivery in Nairobi typically takes 1-2 business days. For other locations in Kenya, delivery takes 3-5 business days. We'll provide tracking information once your order is shipped.
